Frequently Asked Questions

How is the news verified?

Our news is rigorously fact-checked against multiple international scientific journals and reputable media outlets. We provide source links at the end of each article for complete transparency.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes, our core platform, including daily news and blogs, is completely free. We also offer premium e-books and courses for those who wish to dive deeper into specific subjects.

How can I contribute?

We welcome contributions! You can get involved by participating in our Q&A forums, submitting blog post ideas, or joining our volunteer fact-checking team. Visit our 'About Us' page for more details.
